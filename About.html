LIYANA HEALTH CARE PLC

LIYANA HEALTH CARE PLC (LHS) was established ON 2012 G.C after 12 years working in Hawassa and in different part of Ethiopia. We are giving international standard service here in Addis, through our 12 years work experience, we are treated more than two million patients per year with excellence and awarded five times in quality service every consecutive years. With more than 800+ employees and 200+ specialists, over 2 million patients has been served since its opening, Knowing the backwardness of the higher, tertiary and quaternary level treatments in Ethiopia in both private and government setups the concept of having super-specialized Centre was of an emergent need. This is the basic concept of the inception of the super specialized Centre named Yanet Internal Medicine center.
